About

Hi, I'm Tina. I specialize in builder/hard gel and Gel X extensions, focusing on structure, precision, and long-lasting, refined results.

I am also experienced in gel manicures, dip, hybrid/poly gel systems, and acrylics, allowing me to select the most suitable method for each client while maintaining the integrity and appearance of the nails.

My style ranges from minimal, clean aesthetics to modern French, soft ombré, and custom-designed sets — all tailored to complement each client's personal style.

This is a detail-focused, non-rushed service experience designed for clients who value quality, consistency, and well-executed work. Every set is customized to fit your lifestyle and aesthetic.
