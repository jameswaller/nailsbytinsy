About

Detail-focused,
never rushed.

— Tina

I specialize in builder/hard gel and Gel X extensions — structure, precision, and results that last.

I'm also experienced in gel manicures, dip, hybrid/poly gel, and acrylics. This allows me to choose the best method for each client while protecting the integrity of your natural nails.

My style ranges from minimal, clean aesthetics to modern French, soft ombré, and fully custom sets — always tailored to you.

Every appointment is an unhurried, one-on-one experience for clients who value quality, consistency, and well-executed work.

Services

What I offer

Gel X Extensions

Full-cover soft gel tips with builder gel overlay. Lightweight, natural-looking, and gentle on your nails.

Builder / Hard Gel

Sculpted structure for strength and length. Ideal for clients who want durability with a refined finish.

Gel Manicure

Classic gel polish on your natural nails. Clean, glossy, and chip-resistant for 2–3 weeks.

Nail Art Add-On

Custom designs — from French tips and ombré to hand-painted details. Priced per complexity.

Dip Powder

Durable, lightweight powder application with a wide range of color options. No UV lamp needed.

Soak Off / Removal

Gentle removal of any existing set. Includes nail shaping and a hydrating treatment.
